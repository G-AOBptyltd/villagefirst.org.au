## Location

Smiths Lake Village is situated on the shores of Smiths Lake, a pristine coastal lake connected to the Tasman Sea. The village is approximately 300km north of Sydney and 30km south of Forster-Tuncurry.

## Our Community

With around 1,300 permanent residents, our village swells during holiday periods as families return to their holiday homes. We have a strong sense of community, with active volunteer groups and regular community events.

## Natural Environment

The area is renowned for its natural beauty, including:
- Pristine lake waters ideal for kayaking and fishing
- Native bushland with diverse wildlife
- Walking trails through coastal heath
- Nearby Booti Booti National Park
