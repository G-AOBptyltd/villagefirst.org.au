We're excited to launch the new Smiths Lake Village community website at villagefirst.com.au!

## What You'll Find Here

- **Community News & Updates** - Stay informed about what's happening in our village
- **Emergency Information** - Quick access to important contacts and evacuation plans
- **Governance Documents** - PPCA meeting minutes, policies, and reports
- **Planning Information** - Current development proposals and community plans
- **Environment Programs** - Lake health monitoring and conservation initiatives
- **Local History** - Learn about our area's rich heritage

## Get Involved

This website is for our community. If you have content to contribute or suggestions for improvement, please contact the PPCA committee.
