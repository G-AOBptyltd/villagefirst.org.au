About Our Area

Discover Smiths Lake and the Pacific Palms region

Welcome to Smiths Lake Village

Smiths Lake is a picturesque coastal village located in the Great Lakes region of New South Wales, approximately 300 kilometres north of Sydney. The village takes its name from the pristine lake that forms its centrepiece.

Home to approximately 1,300 permanent residents, Smiths Lake swells significantly during holiday periods as visitors discover the area's natural beauty. The community is known for its friendly, village atmosphere and strong environmental consciousness.
